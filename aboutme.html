My name is Sarah Holtman. I'm 19 years old and currently perusing mechanical engineering in college. Apart from engineering, fashion is a huge part of my life and has always been something i am passionate about. I enjoy it's history and vastness for creativity and experimentation. I draw a lot of my inspiration from fashion creators Bloody Osiris, Jadon Grundy, Samuel Norris, and artist, Billie Eilish. I hope to help inspire others in the same way. My dream is to be able to freely create and produce fashion for people.
