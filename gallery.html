Patchwork

Styled Outfits
❮ ❯

As an engineer, I really like technical drawings and patterns which can be seen through these patches. I like this because while making art, I always seek perfection which can take days or weeks, but it always makes the product that much more rewarding. All of these denim patches will be sewn onto jeans as a collection.

1. Snake: This is a black and white snake with green eyes. I made the eyes green because snakes are known for having captivating eyes. I wanted to make this because I think snakes are very interesting. I did some research and found what scale pattern I liked the most to use for the snake.


2. Blohsh: This is a blohsh, Billie Eilish's logo, but I replaced its head with a flame. I really like Billie Eilish, so i wanted to replicate her symbol but add my own take and creativity to it. I think the conflict between the orange and yellow paint really make it pop.


3. Spider: This is an all black spider on a white background for high contrast. This patch took the longest because I wanted to get every leg and every proportion as accurate as possible. I made this patch because I think the geometry of spiders is very interesting. Every little detail is so important for drawing something like this.
